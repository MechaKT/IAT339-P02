P02 Style Guide

This document collects the required HTML elements for a style guide, plus the brand direction for a small Vancouver-based “late night halal bites” brand: Drift & Dine.

Interactive Elements

In-text Link

Example sentence that feels “real”: pickup hours

Sample code:

<p>Our <a href="#hours">pickup hours</a> change during exam season.</p>

In-text Link

Follow us on Instagram, X, and Facebook for updates.

Sample code:

<p>Follow us on
  <a href="https://www.instagram.com/">Instagram</a>,
  <a href="https://x.com/">X</a>,
  and <a href="https://www.facebook.com/">Facebook</a> for updates.
</p>

Navigation Link

Sample code:

<a class="nav-link" href="#menu">Menu</a>

Rendered element:

Menu

Submit / Reset Buttons

Used for confirming an order or clearing the form.

Sample code:

<form action="#" method="post">
  <button type="submit">Place order</button>
  <button type="reset">Clear form</button>
</form>

Rendered element:

Text Input Field with Label

Used for order notes like “no onions” or “extra spicy”.

Sample code:

<label for="order-notes">Order notes</label>
<input id="order-notes" name="order-notes" type="text"
  placeholder="e.g., no onions, extra spicy" />

Rendered element:

Radio Button Input with Label

How the customer wants to receive their food.

Sample code:

<fieldset>
  <legend>Fulfillment</legend>
  <label><input type="radio" name="fulfillment" value="pickup" /> Pickup</label>
  <label><input type="radio" name="fulfillment" value="delivery" /> Delivery</label>
</fieldset>

Rendered element:

Fulfillment

Image as a Link

Featured item detail.

Sample code:

<a href="#product_detail">
  <img src="img/featured-wrap.jpg" alt="Featured: Midnight Wrap" width="280" />
</a>

Rendered element:

Note: Make sure img/featured-wrap.jpg exists in your project.

Basic Branding Ideas

What does the company do / offer?

Drift & Dine is a small halal late-night food spot: warm wraps, bowls, and chai.

Who is the company?

A calm, slightly funny friend who always has snacks in their bag.

Why might someone buy from this company?

Small menu, consistent quality, simple ordering, and late-night hours that match student life.

Company Updates-content hint for P02

  • Jan 20: open until 2AM for late night pickup
  • Jan 27: New menu test 'Smoky Lentil Bowl limited run

Pickup Hours anchor

Mon–Thu: 6PM–1AM • Fri–Sat: 6PM–2AM • Sun: 6PM–12AM

Brand Components

Fonts

  • Primary: Open Sans-body, UI labels
  • Secondary: Open Sans Bold-headings
  • System note: headings are tight body is roomy & readable at night

Colour palettes

palette images as the source-of-truth for colour decisions

How they fit: Grays build the 'structure' readability & hierarchy, blues handle emphasis

Imagery + intended applications

Imagery samples are grouped together

Icon direction- simple line icons, rounded ends, 1.5–2px stroke.

Where used: navigation labels, checkout “steps”, small badges (Halal, Spice, Pickup).
